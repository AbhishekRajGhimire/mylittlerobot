# 🤖 My Little Robot Friend

**Meet your new best friend for your desktop!**

Are you lonely while working? Do you wish you had a tiny companion who lives on your screen, explores your windows, and keeps you company while you type? 

**My Little Robot Friend** is an adorable, fully-interactive desktop pet who breathes life into your computer! 

### ✨ Features That Will Melt Your Heart
* **Parkour Pro:** Watch as he intelligently detects your open application windows and uses them as platforms to walk around and explore your screen.
* **Work Buddy Mode:** When he notices you typing away at your keyboard, he'll pull out his tiny reading glasses and a mini laptop to "work" right alongside you!
* **Interactive Petting:** Give him a scratch on the head with your mouse and watch him get giant heart eyes and purr with happiness. 
* **Customizable Fashion:** Accessorize your robot with a classy Top Hat, a snappy Bow Tie, or cool Sunglasses. You can even use the built-in color picker to paint him your absolute favorite color!
* **Playful Reactions:** Try tossing him across the screen! (Just don't throw him *too* hard, or he'll get dizzy!). If you move your mouse too fast and startle him, he might even run away and play peekaboo from the edge of your monitor!
* **Spontaneous Dance Parties:** Occasionally, he just gets so happy that he has to break out into an 8-bit dance jig.

### 🚀 How to Adopt Your Robot
Getting him set up on your machine is incredibly easy:
1. Clone this repository.
2. Install the required dependencies:
   ```bash
   pip install -r requirements.txt
   ```
3. Bring him to life!
   ```bash
   python main.py
   ```

*(He can't wait to meet you!)*
